Terms & Conditions

1. Quote & Pricing

All quotes are estimates based on the information provided and are subject to change upon site inspection. Final pricing will be confirmed after site evaluation and may vary based on site conditions, local permit requirements, soil conditions, and building modifications requested.

2. Service Area

Cat 5 Steel Buildings serves customers throughout Florida, Georgia, and Alabama (excludes Miami/Dade County).

3. Hurricane Certification

Buildings are rated for 180 MPH Cat 5 hurricane wind speeds when properly installed according to manufacturer specifications and local building codes. Installation must be performed by certified installers.

4. Turnkey Service

Our turnkey service includes permits, concrete pad installation, building delivery, and installation. Concrete pad pricing is flat-rate at $6,500 for standard sizes (up to 30×50). Larger or non-standard pads may require additional charges.

5. Payment Terms

Payment schedules vary based on financing method chosen. Options include:
• Rent-to-own: 20% down payment (building only, does not include concrete or installation)
• Launch Credit Union financing: 8-11% APR for complete package (subject to credit approval)
• Cash payment: Full payment required before installation begins

6. Timeline

Installation timelines vary based on building size, site conditions, weather, and permitting requirements. Typical installations are completed within 2-4 weeks after permits are approved.

7. Cancellation Policy

Deposits are non-refundable once permitting process has begun or materials have been ordered. Full cancellation terms will be detailed in your purchase agreement.

8. Contact Authorization

By submitting your information, you authorize Cat 5 Steel Buildings to contact you via phone, email, or text message regarding your quote, services, and follow-up communications.

9. Warranty

Buildings come with manufacturer warranty as specified by GA Southern Steel. Warranty terms will be provided with your purchase agreement.

10. Changes to Terms

Cat 5 Steel Buildings reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Updated terms will be posted on our website.
