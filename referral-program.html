Steel Building Referral Program

Terms & Conditions

These Terms & Conditions govern participation in the Steel Building Referral Program. By submitting a referral, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms.

1. Program Overview

The Referral Program allows independent participants (“Referrers”) to earn a cash bonus for introducing qualified buyers who purchase a steel building through our company.

Participation in this program does not create an employment, agency, partnership, or joint venture relationship.

2. Referral Bonus Amount

The total referral bonus is $500 per completed building, paid in two stages:

  • $250 when the customer submits a deposit and the order is formally accepted
  • $250 when construction begins (ground break, concrete pour, or building installation, as determined by the company)

3. Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for referral compensation:

  • The referral must be submitted before the buyer has any prior contact with our company
  • The referral must include accurate buyer contact information
  • The buyer must confirm the referrer’s introduction
  • Only one referrer per buyer is eligible (first valid submission only)

4. Prohibited Referrals

The following referrals are not eligible:

  • Self-referrals
  • Referrals involving a spouse, domestic partner, or immediate family member
  • Referrals involving businesses owned or controlled by the referrer
  • Fraudulent, misleading, or misrepresented referrals
Any attempt to abuse or manipulate the referral system may result in removal from the program and forfeiture of unpaid referral compensation.

5. Cancellations & Refunds

If a project is cancelled prior to construction beginning, no further referral compensation is owed beyond any amount already paid.

Referral compensation may be required to be returned only if cancellation results from fraud, misrepresentation, or violation of these Terms & Conditions.

6. Payment Terms

Referral bonuses are paid only after qualifying milestones are met and all funds have cleared. Payment timing may vary based on banking, processing, or project scheduling.

7. Taxes

Referral bonuses are considered miscellaneous income. Referrers are solely responsible for reporting and paying any applicable taxes. A tax form may be issued if required by law.

8. Program Modifications

We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or terminate this Referral Program at any time, with or without notice.
