All quotes are estimates based on the information provided and are subject to change upon site inspection. Final pricing will be confirmed after site evaluation and may vary based on site conditions, local permit requirements, soil conditions, and building modifications requested.

2. Service Area

Cat 5 Steel Buildings serves customers throughout Florida, Georgia, and Alabama (excludes Miami/Dade County).

3. Hurricane Certification

Buildings are rated for 180 MPH Cat 5 hurricane wind speeds when properly installed according to manufacturer specifications and local building codes. Installation must be performed by certified installers.

4. Turnkey Service

Our turnkey service includes permits, concrete pad installation, building delivery, and installation. Concrete pad pricing is flat-rate at $6,500 for standard sizes (up to 30×50). Larger or non-standard pads may require additional charges.

5. Payment Terms

Payment schedules vary based on financing method chosen. Options include:
